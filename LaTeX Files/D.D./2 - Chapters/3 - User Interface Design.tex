\section{Student View}
This section presents the web pages designed for the student users of the platform. \\
The key pages for effective interactions with the platform are the followings:

    \begin{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Sign-Up}: figures \ref{fig:studentsignupA} and \ref{fig:studentsignupB} illustrate the two pages students interact with to complete their registration; these pages, indeed, guide students in providing all the necessary information to access the platform

        \item \textbf{Student Homepage}: upon logging in (see Figure \ref{fig:login}), the student is redirected to his personalized homepage; this page displays the student’s profile information and serves as a central hub for accessing other functionalities (Figure \ref{fig:studentprofile})

        \item \textbf{Internship Search Page}: figures \ref{fig:studentsearchA} and \ref{fig:studentsearchB} demonstrate how students can explore internship opportunities published on the platform; the search functionality even allows users to apply filters for a more targeted search. Figure \ref{fig:studentsearchresult} showcases the results of a sample search, displaying relevant internship listings

        \item \textbf{Sent Applications Page}: the page depicted in Figure \ref{fig:studentsentapplicationview} provides an overview of all applications submitted by the student, including the current status of each application so as to offer a clear summary of the progress for every internship applied to

        \item \textbf{Specific Application Page}: this page offers detailed insights into an individual application. \\
        Figure \ref{fig:studentselectionprocess} shows a student reviewing an application in the "Selection Process" phase, allowing options to complete questionnaires and schedule interviews. \\
        Meanwhile, Figure \ref{fig:studentinternship} highlights an application in the "Internship" phase, where the student can provide feedback on his ongoing experience

        \item \textbf{Feedback Page}: Figure \ref{fig:studentfeedbacksection} presents the feedback section, which displays all feedback exchanged between students and companies, considering both comments and complaints. \\
        Complaints are categorized into "Solved" and "Pending" states, providing a clear view on their resolution status\\
        
    \end{itemize}

\section{Company View}
This section presents the web pages designed for the company users of the platform. \\
The key pages for effective interactions with the platform are the followings:

    \begin{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Sign-Up}: figures \ref{fig:companysignupA} and \ref{fig:companysignupB} illustrate the two pages companies interact with to complete their registration; these pages, indeed, guide companies in providing all the necessary information to access the platform

        \item \textbf{Student Homepage}: upon logging in (see Figure \ref{fig:login}), the company is redirected to his personalized homepage; this page displays the company’s profile information and serves as a central hub for accessing other functionalities (Figure \ref{fig:companyprofile})

        \item \textbf{Company Internship Proposals Page}: The page shown in Figure \ref{fig:companyinternshipproposalsview} offers a comprehensive overview of all internship proposals submitted by the company; each proposal will be displayed along with the corresponding number of applicants.\\ Additionally, this page allows the company to publish new internship proposals

        \item \textbf{Applications List Page}: the page depicted in Figure \ref{fig:companyapplicationlistmanagement} allows the company to view all the applicants for a specific internship proposal; for each applicant, details such as the submission date and associated university are displayed. \\
        Additionally, the page provides access to the description of the internship proposal itself and also offers filtering options, enabling the company to view all applications, only those in the "Selection Process" status or those in the "Internship" status

        \item \textbf{Application Evaluation Page}: when the company opens a specific application, the page shown in Figure \ref{fig:companyapplicationevaluation} is displayed. \\
        This page allows the company to review the student’s CV, update the application’s status and send questionnaires or interview proposals directly to the student.

        \item \textbf{Questionnaire Management Page}: figure \ref{fig:companysentquestionnaire} illustrates the page where the company can manage sent questionnaires; this includes viewing both pending and answered questionnaires, consulting the responses and sending new questionnaires to students as needed\\\\\\\
        
    \end{itemize}
